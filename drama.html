If you have an appreciation for the theater arts then this is the club for you!

It takes more than just acting to put on the performance of a lifetime here at TCC. Our clubs offers a variety of oportunities for students to assist in making our annual production come to life. We welcome those of you who like to act, sing, and dance; as well as those who enjoy working behind the scenes in stage management, costumes and make-up .
