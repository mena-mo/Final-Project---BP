Robotics Club

Our club offers a fun, competitive space where you can improve your building, programming, and notetaking skills, while working to be your best self.

- Patrick Dunn, Robotics Club Sponsor

Welcome to the Robotics Club, where innovation meets creativity!
Our club is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of robotics through hands-on projects, colloaborative learning, exciting competitions, and networking. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or a complete beginner, our club offers something for everyone no matter the skill level. We promote career awareness in hope that we can increase the number of working graduates in the Robotics workforce.
