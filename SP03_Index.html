School Clubs

Get involved in one (or more than one!) of our amazing clubs today! Students have come together to create many different organizations to help enrich student life on campus.

Why?

There are many reasons a student may decide to join a club including:

  • Networking
  • Resumes
  • College Applications
  • Scholarship Opportunities
  • Skill Development
  • Community
  • Fun!

How?
